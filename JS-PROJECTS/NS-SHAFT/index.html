How to play

  • Move the character with arrow keys and dive deeper into the cave.
  • You will die if you fall all the way to the bottom or run out of the "life".
  • "Life" is displayed on the upper left corner of the screen.
  • "Life" decreases when you touch the pillards, but you can recover the "life" guage by landing on the normal floor.
